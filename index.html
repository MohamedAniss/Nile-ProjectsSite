About Us

Nile Projects is a general contracting company founded since 2002. We built our experience in Oil & Gas fields (Mechanical, Electrical, Steel Structures, Instrumentation, and Supplying), we do our works as the standard specification, and we own our experience in studying, estimating and execution for many projects. We develop and execute major projects offering high level of products, services and solutions with a broad range of activities. Nile Projects has established an international network of high qualified and professional principles to meet the need of the fast developing market in the Middle East.
